BERA is a crucial tool for detecting hearing loss in children early on, preventing potential speech, language, cognitive, and emotional challenges

Through BERA, we delve into the intricate workings of the auditory nerve, understanding its ability to transmit sound impulses and pinpointing the type, severity, and location of any hearing irregularities

Beyond its role in hearing assessment, BERA unveils its prowess in otoneurologic diagnosis, uncovering hidden secrets like hearing nerve tumours, brain tumours, and other nerve disorders

As the child dreams peacefully, electrodes are gently placed on the head and behind the ear, paving the way for a seamless BERA experience.

The BERA test ensures a worry-free expedition, as it requires no special preparation, posing no risk or discomfort. With oil-free hair and gentle sedation, if needed, the child can go through the test with ease.
